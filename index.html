NeuroISR: AI-Synthesised One-Time Instruction-Set Randomisation

Prabin Shrestha
Graduate Student  •  McNeese State University  •  University of the Cumberlands

Abstract

We present NeuroISR, the first system that uses a generative transformer to produce instance-specific, semantically equivalent x86-64 instruction streams whose op-codes are one-time unobservable to attackers. No ISA extensions, no keys, no reboots.
